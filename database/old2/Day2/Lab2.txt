Students (#app_no, name[first, last], city, postal_code, birth_date, 
		phone, course_id, course_desc, enrolled, attended)

-app_no is the primary key for the table.
-"enrolled" column is specifically used to indicate whether a student is enrolled in the course or not.
-"attended" column is specifically used to register the number of days a student has attended the course.
-Each student can have multiple phone numbers.
-Each student can enroll in multiple courses.
